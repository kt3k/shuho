OSS

denoland/deno

  • 1.0.0 was released. The release blog revealed many things such as the plan of rewriting TSC with Rust, etc.

    I was acknowledged at the end of the article as well as other major contributors. Maybe this was something I was looking for during following the development of Deno. When I first started using Node.js in 2012 in GREE, the initial development of Node.js looked legendary to me. I wonder what it feels like to join the initial development of such widely used software. That was one of the motivation I joined the development of Deno. Now I was acknowledged as the 6th most contibuted member of Deno. Now I have both very satisfied feelings and very unsatisfied feelings. Maybe I will write about them in some future somewhere.
